Our Story

Pacific Trails Resort is more than just a place to stay; it's an experience. Nestled on the California North Coast, we offer a unique blend of luxury camping and natural beauty. Our commitment is to provide a tranquil escape where guests can immerse themselves in the wonders of the Pacific Ocean.
